Buttons come in five flavours, .button-primary, .button-secondary, .button-white, .button-tertiary and .button-tertiary--dark, and three sizes, .button--l, default (no class required), and .button--s.

You can also set a minimum width, .button--set-min-width, for consistency. This sets a minimum width on larger screens, and a 100% width on small screens.
