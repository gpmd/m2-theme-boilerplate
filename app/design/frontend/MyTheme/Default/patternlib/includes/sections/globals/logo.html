The logo can be added to an element using one of the classes below and the data-grunticon-embed attribute. There are three colour variations, black, grey and white, and three size variations, --l, --m and --s.

Example markup:

<div class="icon__logo--l-black" data-grunticon-embed></div>
icon__logo--l-black
icon__logo--m-black
icon__logo--s-black
